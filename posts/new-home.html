New looks and new home
I originally self-hosted this site on a Raspberry Pi 3b sitting on a trolley next to my desk.

This worked out well for about a year, but due to ongoing intermittent power issues taking out this site every time there is a blip I decided to move it to GitHub Pages.

At some point, adding posts and manually updating the index will become too cumbersome so I'll look into rebuilding the site using Jekyll or Hugo, depending on which makes the most sense to me.

It also felt time to give the site a bit of a facelift too.
